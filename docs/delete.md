# Delete Account

**Cinescenes**

---

## Delete from the app

The quickest way to permanently delete your account and all associated data is directly inside the app:

1. Open Cinescenes
2. Go to **Settings → Delete Account**
3. Confirm the deletion

Deletion is immediate and irreversible. Your name, email, and game history are permanently removed.

---

## Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, email [support@cinescenes.app](mailto:support@cinescenes.app) with the subject line **Delete my account** and we will process your request within 30 days.

---

## What gets deleted

- Your name and email address
- Your game history and scores
- Your account credentials

Anonymised gameplay records (turns, scores) may be retained without any link to your identity, so other players' game histories remain intact.
